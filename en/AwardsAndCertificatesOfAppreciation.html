Awards and certificates of appreciation

The Casablanca Group for Hotel Services and Tourism is one of the hotel tourisms groups that owns several hotels of different degrees and classifications in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, from five stars, four stars and three stars. The group has enjoyed a distinguished reputation in providing all that is new in the world of hotel services due to its keenness on selecting human elements with competence and experience in the management and operation of hotel and tourism projects, as well as its constant keenness to provide hotel services in accordance with the highest standards and quality specifications. The hotel sector, in addition to presenting new ideas in the field of marketing activation for the tourism and service sector, which works to raise the operating rates resulting from tourist and marketing attractions.
