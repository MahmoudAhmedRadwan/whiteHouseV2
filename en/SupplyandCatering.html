About the factory

For more than 20 years, the Casablanca Group has been distinguished for its logistical services, external parties, and the services of the holy sites for the hospitality of pilgrims and Umrah pilgrims, devoting all their capabilities to provide the best hotel services that were characterized by a distinguished reputation in the field of parties, logistics and catering, especially the Hajj seasons. And the Umrah that the group set in mind to improve the quality and level of the services provided, as the group built the Casablanca factory for supply and catering and outdoor party services, which is the first factory of its kind in the Makkah region and was built according to the specifications of this in addition to the production lines that were supplied The factory is in it for production (Iso22000), the international ISO for food quality and safety, hot and dry meals, with external buffet services for different menus and for VIPs to provide a unique diversity in the menus and food services provided, in addition to the huge fleet of cars equipped with heaters and refrigerators To transfer our services to all parts of the holy sites and the western region as well, the group also built its own kitchen in the holy sites in Mina (Wadi Mahsar) to let It provides services for pilgrims, and the services provided by the group during the Umrah and Hajj seasons varied, as the group added its hotel touches during the Hajj seasons by providing some distinctive hotel services such as reception services, internal supervision, and services for supervising the buffet service. Its skilful management of hotel operations in the holy sites has made us at the forefront of hotel groups providing catering and catering services during the Hajj and Umrah seasons.
