Casablanca Azizia Hotel

Reservation

It is one of the Casablanca Group hotels and a continuation of the tourism, hotel and service development projects carried out by the group’s management in the Makkah region. And a suite that was designed according to the highest hotel and service levels that suit the comfort of pilgrims and Umrah pilgrims. The hotel is 15 minutes away from the Holy Mosque and 7 minutes from the holy sites. All the services needed by the pilgrims have been attached throughout the Hajj season.
Umrah is like free internet, a mosque for men and another for women, a restaurant that serves three meals for men and another for women, in addition to providing a transfer service from the hotel to the Holy Mosque during the Umrah and Hajj season. The hotel also includes many banquet and event halls that suit the authentic Arab taste to suit the desire of many Saudi Arabian families. The hotel is in the Aziziyah area along the direct road leading to the Great Mosque of Mecca through the Aziziyah Tunnel. The Grand Mosque is 7 minutes away from it, and the holy sites (Mina - Muzdalifah - Arafat) are approximately 11 minutes. The hotel contains 150 hotel rooms designed In accordance with international hotel standards to suit the desire of the pilgrims and Umrah pilgrims, in addition to hotel services for transporting Umrah pilgrims during the five daily prayers during the Umrah season, as well as the hotel hosting many Hajj companies and official missions from different countries and services Logistics and supplies provided to him at the hotel and the holy sites.
