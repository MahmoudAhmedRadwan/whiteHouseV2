Hotels / Jeddah

Casablanca Grand Hotel

Reservation

The hotel is the latest project of the Casablanca Company for Hotel Services in Jeddah with a new level of 5-star rating and has been designed according to the latest technological standards in the provision of hotel services. The Casablanca Grand Hotel is distinguished by its five-star rating and its strategic location in Jeddah (Bride of the Red Sea) on Madinah Road and its proximity to King Abdulaziz International Airport only 4 minutes and its unique location in front of one of the most famous shopping centers in Jeddah (Mall of Arabia) and away from the terminal airport The foreigner is only 10 minutes away, and it is close to the consulates and the largest commercial companies in the field of finance and business. It is also only 15 minutes from the charming Jeddah Corniche.

Hotel resturants

Hotel halls

Ewan Restaurant

It can accommodate approximately 80 people. It is located in the main hotel lobby area and serves buffet Daily breakfast, lunch and dinner, in addition to a selection of various menus from the masterpieces of the chef of your choice (select menu)

Al Bustan Restaurant

It features a panoramic view of the city of Jeddah. It serves daily a selection of menus by the pool with a variety of fresh juices, distinctive hookah, and oriental and western sweets prepared from several Arab and European countries, during the period from 11 am until 12 am.

Coffee garden

It offers a variety of fresh juices, distinctive hookah, and oriental and western sweets, which were prepared from several Arab and European countries in a traditional atmosphere, during the period from 1 pm until 1 am.

Barista Cafe

It serves daily a selection of fresh juices and hot drinks. And a selection of light menus and desserts with a distinctive view of the city of Jeddah. It can accommodate 50-70 people and is open 24 hours a day.

Lemon grass Restaurant

Panoramic view of the city of Jeddah and offers many Japanese and Chinese specialty and varied menus. It was designed according to distinctive Asian decorations, and the restaurant operates from 4 pm to 12 pm.

Al fursan Hall

Elegant architectural style. In harmony with the luxurious decoration and splendor of design, it was specially designed for men's celebrations and can accommodate approximately 50-80 people. With menus carefully prepared to suit all tastes.

Myflower Hall

Designed according to high-level hotel decor, with a selection of the chef's masterpieces from the most delicious oriental and western menus. The hall can accommodate about 80-200 people with the largest screens in the field of parties to make your party a memorable memory.

Elysee hall

A creation of French and classic decor for a legendary party. With distinctive hospitality and a selection of menus that suit different tastes, the hall can accommodate approximately 240-260 people. It has been equipped according to the latest advanced technology in the field of holding parties and events.

Meeting Room

(U-shape) Designed for business, training, and meeting style with the latest audio-visual technology. It can accommodate about 20-25 people with a selection of light menus and cold drinks.

Hotel services

In the room

Coffee machine

There is a coffee machine in your room with its own instructions for use and it is provided free of charge during the stay.

Jacuzzi

All suites of the hotel are equipped with a Jacuzzi, in addition to the presence of a video TV in the Jacuzzi of the Royal Suites, which adds more relaxation to your stay with us.

Internal supervision

At your service 24 hours a day. Through trained staff according to the latest hotel service performance standards. He is also pleased to serve you in providing all the comforts during your stay with us.

Room service

Selected meals from the chef's masterpieces are served with a selection of the most delicious Arab and international dishes 24 hours a day. If you have a desire for special items, the way of the chefs is happy to serve you.

Internet

The hotel management was keen to provide the best business services during your stay in the hotel, so please inquire about the password from the staff to connect your device to the wireless internet service (free of charge).

Entertainment services

Your room TV has many entertainment services and modern Arab and international movies provided according to their own additional prices, in addition to wake-up services, weather services, currency rates and flight schedules that you can view through the room TV.

In the hotel

The Mosque

The hotel provides a private prayer hall located on the floor (the Qibla sign is also located in your room)

Sports Club

The hotel has a swimming pool on the upper floor (with a great view of Jeddah) and works from 8 am until 12 am.

Customer Service Department

The main lobby information team is happy to serve you throughout the day. By arranging tourist trips or inquiring about everything related to the city's tourist attractions, as well as securing transfer services to and from the airport and many of your inquiries during the stay at the hotel.

Washing machine

The hotel management provides cleaning services, steam iron and cleaning service during your stay. Using the latest express sterilizers and steamers, according to a high-class hotel style.

Kids Club

The hotel management has been keen to provide a lot of entertainment for your children with the presence of the service staff to take care of your children throughout the stay in the hotel

Business Service Center

The business center operates from 9 am until midnight and provides secretarial services, sending and receiving e-mails, faxes, printing and photocopying of documents, in addition to private computers with high-speed Internet. Also, our staff is qualified to receive all your daily work and help you complete it with great care.
